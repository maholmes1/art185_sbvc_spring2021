Things to do and see

Eastern Kiribati includes the Line Islands, including Kiritimati aka Christmas Island, the world's largest atoll. It offers beaches, world class fishing, scuba diving and birdwatching; Ronton (London), the main settlement of Kiritimati, is a port of call for cruises.

In the west is the Gilbert Islands, which offer amazing and unique cultural experiences. The nation's capital of Tarawa has historic sites and artefacts where one of the bloodiest and most famous battles of World War II, the Battle of Tarawa, was fought. In South Tarawa there is the water park known as the Ambo Lagoon Club.
